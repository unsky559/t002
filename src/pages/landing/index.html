The purchase of tokens will allow the holder to purchase real Tesla products at a discount, use the TESLA token as a payment tool or access to the limited services offered by the company. Last, but not least, the holder can stake TESLA tokens and earn extra TESLA tokens.

Roadmap

The history of Tesla dates back to 1946. The modern appearance of the company has been formed since 2012, when the brand returned to the Czech Republic. Now the company connects a trusted brand, smart home and the world of cryptocurrencies. In the near future, we plan to enter most EU markets and further expand our products to other major countries.
